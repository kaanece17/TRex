# Invalidated Runs

The earlier 2024 subset runs are smoke tests only and must not be treated as strategy backtest results.

Reasons:
- The universe was a manually selected current subset, not full XUSIN.
- The run started in 2024 instead of 2020.
- `shares_outstanding` used current yfinance shares instead of point-in-time KAP `ifrs-full_IssuedCapital`.
